Our Story

A hidden gem on the Philippine coast

Lawis Beach is a pristine coastal escape blessed with crystal-clear waters, golden shores, and breathtaking sunsets that paint the sky in hues of amber and crimson every evening.

Nestled away from the bustle of city life, Lawis Beach offers an authentic slice of island living — from traditional bancas gliding across the bay to the warm smiles of locals who call this place home.

Experience

Everything you love about the sea

🌊

Crystal Waters

Swim, snorkel, or simply float in the calm, crystal-clear waters of Lawis Bay — perfect for all ages and skill levels.

🌅

Stunning Sunsets

Every evening the sky transforms into a canvas of gold, orange, and crimson — a daily spectacle you will never forget.

⛵

Banca Boat Rides

Hop aboard a traditional Filipino outrigger banca and explore the coastline, fishing spots, and nearby coves at your own pace.

🍤

Fresh Seafood

Taste the ocean's bounty — from grilled fish to kinilaw — served fresh daily by local vendors right on the shore.

📷

Photo Paradise

Every corner of Lawis Beach is a perfect shot. Share your memories with our community gallery and discover others' perspectives.

🏕️

Nipa Huts & Rest

Relax in the shade of traditional nipa huts right on the beach — listening to the waves, feeling the breeze, living slow.
